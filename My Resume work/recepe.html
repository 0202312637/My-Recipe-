Ingredient

  • 2 cup of rice
  • 1 cups of tomatoes sauce
  • 1 onios
  • 1/2 cups of vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon of pepper
  • 2 cups of chicken broth

Steps to prepare jallof rice

  1. Heat oil in a pot and try fry oninons until golden
  2. Add tomatoes sauce and cook for 10 minutees.
  3. pour in the rice, stir and cover.
  4. Cook on low heat until rice is soft.
  5. Serve hot with grilled chicken or fish

Chose jallof rice because it is a popular and delicious dish in west Afrrica, enjoyed at parties and family gathering in the Northen part of Ghana and West Africa at large.
